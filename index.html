Scrollytelling + Spec-Driven Development

I used this page as a small field test: can a clear process improve quality in one short build session? The answer was yes.

1. Define Before Generate

Instead of asking AI to "make a cool site," I wrote requirements in small, testable chunks. This made every output reviewable.

2. Phase the Work

Phases kept the scope small enough to verify. I could stop drift early and keep momentum instead of rewriting everything later.

3. Use the Scroll as Narrative

The page tells a sequence, not just a list. Visitors move through a point of view, then branch into detailed content pages.

4. Extend the Technique

This is where scrollytelling becomes practical: use it for the top-level story, then route into deeper pages when readers need specifics.
